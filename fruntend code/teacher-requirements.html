Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our teacher recruitment process

Who can apply to teach through Huffaz?

We welcome applications from qualified teachers with appropriate education credentials and a passion for Islamic education. Requirements vary by position but generally include relevant degrees or certifications.

How long does the application process take?

The initial application takes about 20-30 minutes to complete. After submission, schools typically respond within 2-4 weeks if they're interested in moving forward with your application.

What documents do I need to apply?

You'll need your resume/CV, degree certificates, teaching certifications (if any), CNIC copy, and reference letters. Some positions may require additional documentation.

Can I apply to multiple schools?

Yes! Your profile will be visible to all our partner schools. You can also apply directly to specific positions at different schools through our portal.

Is there a fee to use this service?

No, our teacher recruitment service is completely free for educators. Schools pay a nominal fee to access our network of qualified teachers.

What support do you offer after placement?

We provide ongoing professional development opportunities, networking events, and resources to help you succeed in your teaching position.
